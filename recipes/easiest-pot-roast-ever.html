Easiest Pot Roast Ever!

Description

Here we have a Delicious and easy way to slow cook your way, with very minimal effort, to this pot roast! Just be careful and try not to get too addicted.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium Russet Potatoes
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 1/2 cups of baby carrots
  • 1 (3 pound) beef roast
  • 3 cubes beef bouillon
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes, onions, and celery into fairly large chunks; place in the bottom of a slow cooker along with baby carrots.
  2. Place roast on top of vegetables. Place 3 bouillon cubes randomly on the top of the roast and pour in 1/2 cup of water.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours or on High for 4 to 5 hours. An instant-read thermometer inserted in the thickest part of the roast should read 145-degrees F(65 degrees C).
  4. Transfer roast to a platter. Remove vegetables using a splotted spoon and arrange them around the roast.
